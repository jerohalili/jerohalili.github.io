### The Challenge
Visual artists need a digital space that doesn't distract from their work. Most portfolio builders are either too rigid or too slow, resulting in "layout shift" that ruins the viewing experience of high-resolution art.

### The Solution
I developed a custom **Astro-based portfolio engine**. By utilizing Astro’s View Transitions and a custom masonry grid logic, I created a "gallery-first" experience that feels like a native app rather than a traditional website.

### Technical Highlights
- **Performance:** Achieved a perfect 100/100 Lighthouse score for Performance and Best Practices.
- **Motion:** Integrated Framer Motion for subtle, non-intrusive entrance animations.
- **Optimization:** Used Astro's `<Image />` component to automatically serve WebP formats based on the visitor's device.
